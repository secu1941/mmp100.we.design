Animation Project

Sunrise Animation

I decided to animate a calm and uplifting feeling through movement, colors, and scale. I have multiple layers: the blue background, the brown position moving mountains, and the 3 birds, each on its own layer, that position, move, and rotate at different points in time. The orange sun starts low near the mountains, changing its position upward and scaling bigger as the blue birds fly closer. The blue background and orange sun create a warm contrast, while the moving birds and mountains create motion and depth.
