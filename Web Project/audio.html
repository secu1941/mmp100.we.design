Audio Project

This sound is of going through the motions of finding peace of mind through community and personal grieving. This story starts with a woman alone in her car listening to an instrumental written by herself. This music reminds her of her feelings when she wrote it. Then she proceeds to get out of her car to enter a church, where she finds a community grieving her same loss and celebrating this person. This audio is compiled of 8 different sounds, two of them being my personal recording, all contributing to the concept of silence and listening being comfort for pain. All of the sounds I used were human and very natural. I think the most challenging part was editing the cuts or split cuts to sound seamless.
